Section 01
Brand DNA

Everything in this system extends from a single approved hero lock. Not around it. Not beside it. From within it — drawing from its energy, structure, and momentum to create a coherent, immersive world.

"Ascent Together is not a generic company conference. It is a rallying moment. The brand world must feel like it's in motion — like the whole organization is leaning forward, climbing, converging on something meaningful. Warm. Bold. Earned. Never slick for its own sake."

↑
Upward Momentum
Every layout, composition, and typographic treatment should carry a sense of upward motion. Angles rise. Layers stack. Nothing sits flat. The Ascent chevron mark is the visual engine driving all spatial decisions.
◈
Dimensional Warmth
The hero lock lives in 3D space. Shadows, highlights, and layered ribbon forms give it mass. The system carries this into every touchpoint — depth through shadow, warmth through the flame gradient, texture in print production.
⬡
Two Brands, One Voice
Huddle House and Perkins exist within the Ascent Together event identity as partners, not competitors. The system gives each brand equity without fragmenting the convention's singular visual authority.

Section 03
Type System

Three roles. Clear hierarchy. The display face mirrors the hero lock's condensed, stacked authority. The headline face extends it into functional communication. Body type provides warmth and readability in longer-form content.

Display — Bebas Neue · Hero moments, section titles, environmental lettering
ASCENT TOGETHER 2026
Headline — Barlow Condensed ExtraBold / 700-900 · Subheads, labels, speaker names, slide titles
Our Brands. One Future.
Eyebrow / Overline — Barlow Condensed SemiBold · All-caps, wide tracking
Keynote Address · Main Stage · 9:00 AM
Body — Barlow Light / Regular · Prose, program descriptions, internal comms
Ascent Together is our annual leadership convention bringing Huddle House and Perkins teams together to align on strategy, celebrate wins, and chart the path forward for our brands. This year, we gather in North Carolina with one shared purpose: to rise together.
Typographic Rules
Size Relationships
Display: 48–120pt environmental, 32–72pt slide title. Headline: 14–40pt. Eyebrow: 9–13pt, always 0.25–0.35em tracking. Body: 13–16pt, never smaller than 11pt in print.
Color on Type
Display type takes the flame gradient as a fill on dark backgrounds, or Warm White reversed. Never place gradient type on light backgrounds. Eyebrows are always Flame Amber, never gradient.
Stacking Logic
Headline stacking should echo the hero lock: tight leading (0.9–1.05), full caps, flush left. Never center-align blocks of more than 3 lines. Left margin anchoring creates a consistent reading spine across all materials.
Section 04
Graphic Devices & Supergraphics

The system uses four core graphic devices derived from the hero lock's geometry. These are not decorations — they are structural tools that create space, depth, and energy across every surface.

Ascent Chevron — Primary Mark
Diagonal Slash Band — Momentum Device
Layered Ribbon System — Type Container
Corner Chevron Crop — Corner Anchor
Vertical Flame Bars — Margin & Divider
Full-Bleed Flame — Hero Backgrounds
Usage & Application Rules
Scale with Intent
Supergraphics should always extend beyond their container. Never contain a chevron fully within a frame — crop it dramatically. The impression of infinite scale reinforces upward momentum.
Layering Logic
Darker elements always sit behind lighter gradient elements. Shadow falls downward and to the right to echo the hero lock's 3D perspective. Never reverse the shadow direction.
Flame Bar Protocol
Vertical flame bars are used only as margin devices, column separators, or editorial pull-quote accents. They appear in groups of 1–3, descending in opacity. Never use more than 3.
Section 05
Logo & Lock-up Rules

The hero lock is the approved master asset and must never be redrawn, re-typed, or recreated from scratch. All usage must use supplied production files. These rules govern how it co-exists with brand partner logos and event sub-elements.

Approved Hero Lock — Do Not Alter
Clear Space
Minimum clear space equals the cap-height of the "A" in ASCENT on all four sides. In large-format applications, double this. The hero lock must never be placed within 20px of any edge in digital contexts.
Sub-Brand Placement
Huddle House and Perkins logos appear flanking the Ascent chevron mark, as shown in the approved lock. In standalone event materials (badges, signage, agendas), they may appear as separate co-brand bugs in the footer zone, at 60% the scale of the event lock.
Don't — Prohibited Alterations
❌ Re-type any element of the lock ❌ Change gradient direction or colors ❌ Remove the "2026" from the primary lock ❌ Place on low-contrast backgrounds ❌ Rotate or skew the lock ❌ Apply drop shadows to the lock itself ❌ Surround with competing graphic devices ❌ Use on busy photographic backgrounds
Section 06
Keynote Stage & Lighting

The main stage is the highest-impact moment of the convention. The visual environment must feel like the hero lock come to life in three dimensions — warm, dramatic, and alive with energy.

LED Wall Direction
Center LED displays the hero lock on a deep charcoal background with a slow, breathing radial glow in Flame Amber. Side panels run animated vertical flame bars. During speaker moments, the lock recedes to 15% opacity behind speaker content.
Lighting Design
Key wash: warm amber-orange (Rosco 321 or equivalent). Stage floor: deep red accent wash from downstage. Chevron gobo projected on upstage cyc. Uplighting on any columns or architectural elements in Flame Deep or Flame Orange.
Stage Deck & Scenic
Recommended: matte charcoal stage deck surface. Podium in charcoal with gradient front panel. Flanking totems or pillars in Ascent Smoke with gradient-edged vertical flame bars. 3D dimensional chevron prop as stage focal point.
Section 07
PowerPoint & Deck Templates

Five core slide layouts cover every presentation need. Dark-first design ensures they read powerfully on conference screens. A light variant set is provided for print distribution.

Title Slide — Dark
Section Header — Full Flame
Content + Data — Dark
Quote / Pull Callout — Light
Template Variants
5 layouts: Title, Section Header, Content+Visual, Data/Chart, Quote/Callout. Each has a Dark and Light variant. All include footer zone with event lock, page number, and session watermark.
Animation Style
Wipe-from-left on all body content. Flame bar "reveal" on slide headers — the gradient bar sweeps across before type appears. Transition between slides: push-up with 0.3s ease. No dissolves, no spins, no 3D effects.
Speaker Deck Rules
All speaker-submitted content must use the approved template. Creative team reviews all decks 5 business days before the event. No external brand elements or off-template slides permitted on main stage.

Sign Types
Primary directional totems (freestanding, double-sided, 24"×60"), Room ID signs (mounted, 18"×24"), Schedule boards (rolling or fixed, 24"×36"), Registration banner (8'×2.5'), Step-and-repeat (8'×8' or 10'×8').
Materials
Foam board or Sintra for interior. Aluminum composite (Dibond) for permanent or high-traffic. Matte laminate throughout. No gloss finish — it creates glare on screens and photography. Flame gradient header always printed as spot process.
Wayfinding Language
Room names are short and bold: MAIN STAGE, BREAKOUT A–D, REGISTRATION, HOSPITALITY, AWARDS HALL. Never use just numbers. Session names appear in smaller body weight below. Directional language: always "→ Turn Right," not "East" or abstract descriptors.
Section 10
Digital Screens

Digital touchpoints span lobby displays, mobile event app UI, social countdown graphics, and real-time recognition screens. Animated loops carry the energy of the hero lock into motion.

Lobby Totem
Social / App Header
Wide LED Ticker / Welcome Loop
Loop Animation
Ambient loops: 15s or 30s. Gradient sweeps from Flame Gold to Flame Deep, slow and breathing. Chevron pulses subtly at 0.5 opacity. No hard cuts — all transitions are slow fades or wipes. Speaker slides use a 5s hold then slow pan-up.
Recognition Screens
Awards and franchise milestone recognition use the full-flame background with the attendee's name in Bebas Neue Display at maximum scale. Brand logo of their franchise floats above. High-drama, cinematic moment. Print still versions for the awards dinner program.
Spec Formats
Lobby totem: 1080×1920px. Main stage center: 1920×1080 (16:9). Side panels: 1080×1920. Ticker: 1920×280. Social: 1080×1080 and 1080×1920 Stories. All at 72dpi minimum; stage content at 120dpi minimum.

Merch & Swag Direction

Garment Spec
Primary colorway: Charcoal (#1A1A1A equivalent). Secondary: Cream/Natural. All graphic application by screen print (1–3 color) or direct-to-garment for complex gradient elements. Embroidery for caps and lapels.
Production Notes
Gradient prints require pantone-matched spot inks in sequence. Minimum order: 50 units per style for cost efficiency. Notebooks: debossed cover with foil-stamped spine band. Tumblers: laser-engrave or wraparound shrink-sleeve print.
Distribution Tiers
All-attendee: tote, notebook, pin. Leadership: add hoodie or quarter-zip, premium tumbler. Speaker/Executive: full kit in branded box, plus a numbered enamel pin. On-site gifting in Ascent-branded kraft tissue wrap.
Section 12
Print Collateral & Communications

Internal comms, save-the-dates, printed programs, and awards materials must feel connected to the visual system without being over-branded. The system allows for light-background warmth in print while maintaining dark-first energy in digital.

Save the Date Card — 5"×7" Flat
Official Program Cover — 8.5"×11" Saddle-Stitched
Email Template Header — 600px wide
Award Certificate — 11"×8.5" Landscape · Foil Available
Print Finishes
Gradient elements: spot UV or foil stamp on premium applications (certificates, speaker gifts, program covers). Body text: uncoated matte stock throughout. Never gloss-coat the full print — it creates cheap visual weight. Silk or satin finish where a sheen is needed.
Email System
Header always dark, 600px wide. Body zone can be light or dark, consistent per campaign. Footer: charcoal with Ascent lock at 40% opacity. All communications use Barlow as web-safe system font fallback. Line height: 1.7 on body copy for readability.
Internal Comms
Internal memos, recap decks, and post-event reports use the Light PowerPoint template. Headers carry the flame gradient accent bar. Branded footer on all pages. No external brand coloring in operational documents — reserve the full visual system for public-facing materials.
